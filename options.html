Merid

Passive English vocabulary learning while you browse Vietnamese websites.

AI context check

Optional. When on, the extension asks a language model whether an English word truly fits the sentence before showing it. Only a single sentence is ever sent — never the whole page. No API key is stored inside the extension bundle.

Deploy the Cloudflare Worker in /backend and paste its URL here. See the README.

Bring-Your-Own-Key is for personal / development use. Your key is stored only in chrome.storage.local on this device and is sent directly from your browser to the provider. It is your key and your billing. Not recommended for a shared/published install — use the backend proxy for that.

Saving a Gemini key will ask Chrome for permission to contact generativelanguage.googleapis.com. Use a standard AI Studio key (starts with AIza…).

Replacement

Replace = swap the word. Highlight = keep the original, show meaning on hover. Beside = từ (word).

Data & privacy

  • Page text is scanned locally in your browser to find vocabulary matches.
  • With the AI context check on, only the single sentence around a matched word is sent to your backend/OpenAI — never full pages.
  • Your deck, mastered words, and settings are stored on your device (chrome.storage).
  • Turn the context check Off above to send nothing off-device.
